yayoi kusama



Her Personal History: Since 1977, Yayoi Kusama has been living voluntarily in a psychiatric hospital in Tokyo and works in a nearby studio. Her art often reflects her experiences with hallucinations and psychological disorders, which gives it a unique depth and expression. Her Artistic Style: Her art focuses on themes such as repetition, obsession, and infinity. She is one of the most important living artists in the world, and exhibitions of her work are held in the most famous museums around the globe.
